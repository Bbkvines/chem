About Chem Notes

A personal documentation hub for chemistry notes & reference material.

Hi! I'm a chemistry student who built this site to organise my notes, revision material, and reference content in one clean place. Rather than scattered PDFs and notebooks, everything lives here — searchable, readable, and accessible anywhere.

The site covers Organic, Inorganic, Physical, and Analytical chemistry — from reaction mechanisms to thermodynamics to spectroscopy. Notes are written in plain language with tables, examples, and exam tips where useful.

What you'll find here

Concise notes on core topics, comparison tables, worked examples, and quick-reference callouts. Everything is written to be easy to scan — not a textbook, but a study companion.
